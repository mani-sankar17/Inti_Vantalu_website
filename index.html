ABOUT

"At INTI VANTALU, we understand that good food is essential for a healthy life, especially for students and professionals who relocate to different cities for education or work. Many rely on PGs or hostels, where quality food is often uncertain. That's why we've stepped in to provide delicious, home-like meals delivered straight to their doorsteps, ensuring that they have access to nutritious and comforting food, just like home, no matter where they are."

~ Inti Vantalu

Contribute

We have a famous Telugu proverb: 'Anni daanala kanna annadaanam minna,' meaning 'Among all charities, feeding the hungry is the greatest.' With nearly 1,000 people dying of hunger every hour, we decided to take action. We set aside 10% of our daily earnings to cook, pack, and donate food to the poor and needy, aiming to make a small but meaningful impact.We would greatly appreciate your support in addressing this cause. Even contributing just 1 rupee from every 10 can help make a difference and provide meals for those in need.
